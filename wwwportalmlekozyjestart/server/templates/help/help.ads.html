Advertising on Biostar

Advertising on Biostar is open to active users wishing to promote ideas, products and accomplishments that relate to the focus of the site: Bioinformatics and Genomics.

Who may run ads?

Depending on the accumulated reputation users have the following options to run their ads:
  • Users with reputation of 1000 or above (accumulated after 100 votes) may run one ad free of charge.
  • Users with reputation of 3000 or above (accumulated after 300 votes) may run two ads free of charge.

What may be advertised by Biostar users?

Individual users may advertise personal accomplishments, research results, research publications, tools and techniques that they have developed, blogs or websites that they have created, activities that they undertake regardless of whether these have any commercial nature.
The sole restriction of the user initiated advertising is that the person creating the ad must be legally entitled to claim full or partial ownership (at a non trivial level) of the the product/idea/concept that is being advertised.
For advertising outside these conditions please contact the site manager at admin@biostars.org.

Creating an Ad

To create a new ad select the New Post button and select the Ad category. The title of your post will constitute the line that will be shown as the ad. The body of your posts will be a regular Biostar post that users can access after clicking the link and should contain further information on the advertised product. Note that ads will not show among the other posts of the site.
Ads have two states Stopped and Running Upon creating your ad it will have the Stopped status and it will need to be started to be shown within the Ad rotation. Ads in the Running state will be shown in the top bar.

Starting and Stopping Ads

To see a list of all ads visit the ad list page also accesible from the navigation bar on the top right. For each ad we will show actions that a current user may take or the ‐ symbol if the user may not interact with the ad.
Note: A user may choose to start running their own ads or another user's ads! What this means that a user that wants to run an ad but but does not meet the reputation requirements may ask another user to run their ad. Be advised that regardless of who started the ad only the owner or a moderator may stop an ad.
The creator of an Ad may stop it at any time. Moderators that wish to stop an ad can do so by closing the post that corresponds to the Ad. This will stop the ad moreover the ad can only be run once the post has been opened.

Ad Rotation

We randomly select one ad from the pool of ads. Due to the caching it may take up to 5 minutes for an activated ad to enter the ad rotation.
Users with reputation that is high enough to start an ad may also disable ad display altogheter from their account page.

Expiration

Ads expire after one month and at that point will be placed in the Stopped state. Users will need to reactivate the ad.

Commercial Sponsorship

We expect to reserve about 50% of the ad rotation to Biostar users. We will offer the rest of the ad space to commercial entities in return of supporting the continuing development and maintenance of Biostar. Contact information: admin@biostars.org.
